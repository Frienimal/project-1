About Us

We are a group of engineers who just want to ease the process of pet adoption by making it completely online and free. Animals deserve care and love as well and we make sure to provide that to them. We also want to educate and make people aware of the rising cases of animal assaults in the country and remind every citizen of their duty to protet the environment and everyone within it.
